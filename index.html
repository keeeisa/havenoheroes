Have No Heroes is a punk rock band from Riga, Latvia, formed in 2016. Known for their high-energy performances and socially conscious lyrics, they blend  hardcore with early nineties skate punk, creating a powerful and resonant sound.

Fast punk rock
from riga, latvia

Their music features fast, aggressive guitar riffs and thought-provoking messages that resonate with a global fan base. With albums like "Plastic World" and "Letters to Nowhere," Have No Heroes continuously push genre boundaries, avoiding clichés and inspiring change through their relentless passion and dedication.
